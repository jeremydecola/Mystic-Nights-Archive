Mystic Nights Archive

Welcome, stranger. You’ve uncovered the lost archive of Mystic Nights—
the most mysterious PS2 survival horror game ever released. October 20, 2005. Sony Computer Entertainment Korea. Synopsis: A Korean-Made Survival Horror Adventure for PlayStation®2.
Bruce McKellen, a solitary biology professor, receives a cryptic letter from a long-lost friend.
Allen’s message beckons him to a secret research facility, isolated in the southern seas.
Enclosed: a bizzare photograph—rekindling thoughts and feelings Bruce thought were gone forever.
Compelled by curiosity—and dread—Bruce steps into a forgotten world where buried memories, danger, and the unknown await.
Sometimes, the past refuses to stay dead…
